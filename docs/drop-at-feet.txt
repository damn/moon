Drop item at player's feet — change log
======================================
Date: 2026-07-08

SUMMARY
-------
Ground drop and cursor preview no longer use mouse position or click-distance
clamp. Items spawn and preview at the player's body center (:body/position).

WHY
---
- Simpler inventory/sim boundary: drop no longer needs :ctx/world-mouse-position
  or :entity/click-distance-tiles in item-place-position.
- Removes "throw over hill / out of sight" behaviour (old comment in
  item_place_position.clj).
- Aligns with inventory/sim separation discussion: explicit drop at feet instead
  of frame-dependent placement math.

IN GAME
-------
- Hold item (item-on-cursor state), left-click map (not UI) → item on ground
  under player.
- Ghost preview while dragging shows at feet, not at cursor.

NOT CHANGED
-----------
- FSM :player-item-on-cursor and state-exit still perform ground spawn.
- Inventory put still dissoc's before :dropped-item to avoid double spawn.
- Click world still required (k-handle-input); only position changed.
